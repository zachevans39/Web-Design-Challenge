Wind Speed

Throughout all of the cities analyzed the majority of the cities experice a wind speed from 0mph-5mph, no matter their proximity to the equator. Some cities that have wind speeds beyond 25mph are farther away from the equator as these are cities that expericene low temperatures. It can be seen that Wind Speeds are not dependent on the equator.
