Summary: Latitude vs. [Climate Factors]


The purpose of this project is to observe and analyze different climate factors and how those factors can affect over 500 different cities across the globe. This is done by chosing cities with different distances from the equator. First we gathered data pulled from the OpenWeatherMapApi to assemble a dataset +500 cities

After the datasets were assemblemed I Used Matplotlib to plot various aspects of the weather vs.latitude. The factors we are anazlyzing include:temperature,cloudiness,windspeed, and humidity
